    Status

    The FreeBSD/ppc is still a Tier2 platform.That means it is not being fully supported by our security officer,
    release engineers and toolchain maintainers.

    Latest News
  • 03 April, 2009:Support for the powerPC G5 has been commited to SVN.
  • 03 March, 2008:Support for Freescale® PowerQUICC III MPC85XX family system-on-chip
    development boards has been committed into CVS.
  • 27 Febuarary, 2008:FreeBSD 7.0 is the first release to officially support the FreeBSD/ppc port.
